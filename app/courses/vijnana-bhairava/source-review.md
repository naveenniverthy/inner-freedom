# Vijnana Bhairava Source Review Checklist

For every dharana, confirm:

1. Correct verse number
2. Correct Sanskrit
3. Correct transliteration
4. Plain English rendering is clearly marked as rendering, not literal translation
5. Practice instructions do not exaggerate the original text
6. No mystical claims are added
7. No medical or psychological claims are made
8. No copyrighted commentary is copied
9. Jaideva Singh may be acknowledged as a reference, but commentary should be original
10. Any uncertain entry must remain marked as “needs-review”
